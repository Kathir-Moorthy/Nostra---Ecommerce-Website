Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets like PayPal, Google Pay, and Paytm.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3-5 business days, depending on your location. Express shipping options are available during checkout.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, you can return or exchange items within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please refer to our return policy for more details.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order using the tracking link provided in the shipping confirmation email. You can also check the status by logging into your account.
